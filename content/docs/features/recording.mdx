title: Recording proxy
description: Capture real HTTP traffic from your machine. (Pro)

> **Pro feature.** Available on Pro and Lifetime plans.

The recording proxy spins up a local TLS-terminating proxy and routes any
app on your machine through it. You get real fixtures from real traffic.

## Start a recording

1. Open **Record → New session**.
2. Click **Trust certificate** (a one-time keychain prompt).
3. Pick the apps to capture (Safari, your dev server, a CLI tool).
4. Hit **Start**.

Everything those apps emit shows up in the timeline live.

## Convert to a collection

Select any subset of captured requests and click **Save as collection**.
Koala groups them by host, dedupes near-identical calls, and prompts for
a name.

## What's NOT captured

- Local-only traffic (`localhost` to `localhost`) — opt-in via settings.
- Apps that use cert pinning (most banking apps).
- iOS / Android device traffic — use the Network Link Conditioner profile
  to route a device through your Mac.
