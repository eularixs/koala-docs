## Pricing FAQ

**Q: Can I use Koala commercially?**
A: Yes. Both Free and Pro have no commercial restrictions. You can use Koala at work, build APIs you sell, etc.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: No. Koala is offline-first. License validation runs once per 90 days, cached locally — works offline in between.

**Q: What happens if I cancel?**
A: There's nothing to cancel. One-time purchase = permanent license. You keep the app forever, including all v0.x + v1.x updates.

**Q: Can I transfer my license to a new Mac?**
A: Yes. Settings → License → Deactivate on the old Mac. Reactivate on new. 3-device limit applies (resets every 12 months).

**Q: Team pricing?**
A: Buy multiple individual licenses. We don't do per-seat enterprise pricing — that pricing model funds bullshit overhead, not the product.

**Q: Why not free Pro for open-source?**
A: 50% educational/OSS discount → $7.50. We need to fund development; even a small fee filters out the "free everything" crowd that demands unlimited support without contributing.
