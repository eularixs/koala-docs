Koala is a **native macOS** API client. Built in Swift, runs as a single ~30 MB
app, no Electron, no telemetry, no monthly subscription. Your collections live
on your disk, your secrets in the macOS Keychain.

<Callout type="info">
  **New here?** Skip to [Install in 2 minutes](/docs/getting-started) →
</Callout>

## Why Koala

<Cards>
  <Card title="Fast" description="Cold-start under 300 ms on Apple Silicon. Sending a request feels instant. No spinning beachballs." />
  <Card title="Local-first" description="All data in ~/Library/Application Support/Koala. No accounts required for solo work. Pro users opt in to cloud sync." />
  <Card title="Secure by default" description="Secrets in macOS Keychain. No telemetry. No analytics SDKs. No 'AI assistant' phoning home." />
  <Card title="Keyboard-driven" description="Cmd+T new tab, Cmd+Enter send, Cmd+F find. Move between requests with arrows. Power users feel at home." />
</Cards>

## Pricing at a glance

- **Free** — REST, GraphQL, WebSocket/SSE, scripts, mock server. Unlimited
  collections + requests.
- **Pro $15 one-time** — Recording proxy with HTTPS MITM, automation scheduler,
  role-based collab, beta channel early access.

See the [full pricing breakdown](/docs/pricing).
