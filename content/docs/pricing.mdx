## Licensing

Each Pro license activates up to 3 devices, easily transferable via
your account dashboard. You can deactivate a device at any time from
[the dashboard](/admin) or from the macOS app at **Settings → License**.

Licenses are RSA-signed receipts that the app verifies offline.
Internet is required for activation and once every 90 days for
revalidation.

## Updates

All updates within v0.x and v1.x are included with your Pro purchase.
